Shiro

Classic Ethiopian chickpea stew

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shiro powder
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp berbere
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water

Instructions

  1. SautÃ© onions in oil until soft.
  2. Add garlic and berbere, stir gently.
  3. Mix shiro powder with water separately.
  4. Slowly add while stirring.
  5. Simmer until smooth and thick.
