Doro Wot

Ethiopiaâ€™s iconic spicy chicken stew

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cleaned
  • 4 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp berbere
  • 3 tbsp niter kibbeh
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook onions slowly until deeply browned.
  2. Add niter kibbeh and berbere.
  3. Add chicken and simmer gently.
  4. Add garlic and salt.
  5. Add boiled eggs before serving.
